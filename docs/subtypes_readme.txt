# Subtype assignments EV3

Table of subtypes assigned to each position in the deep landscape dataset (EV2) by our algorithm

## Columns
cluster: Assigned subtype, denoted Xi for subtype i of amino acid X, with i=O for outliers and i=P for permissive positions
study: Study the position came from
gene: Gene
position: Position in the gene (relative to cannonical Uniprot sequence)
wt: Wild-type amino acid
